What a Waste.

We consume more now than ever, but do we obtain the products' full worth? Are they sustainable? When we want an egregious amount more than what we actually need, we end up throwing away resources that may never end up recycled. Instead, they are stashed and hidden away in landfills. These landfills, that might as well be mountains, grow bigger by the second and will one day shadow everything.
